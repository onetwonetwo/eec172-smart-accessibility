Project Overview

The Smart Accessibility Assistant (branded as "Access Assist") is an innovative communication device designed to help individuals with speech disabilities communicate more effectively and affordably. Our working prototype demonstrates real-world functionality with an intuitive menu system displaying essential communication options including "I need water", "I need help", "I'm okay", "Call family", and "Emergency!" - providing users with quick access to their most critical communication needs.
